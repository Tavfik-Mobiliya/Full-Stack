PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
Project Title: Interior & Furniture Design Digital Showroom
Objective: Develop a multilingual (English, Arabic, Turkish) portfolio website for a luxury interior and furniture design company.
Target Users: High-end clients, Homeowners, Interior design enthusiasts, Admin users.

Functional Requirements:
- Multilingual Support (EN, AR, TR) with RTL support for Arabic.
- Homepage: Hero, Dual category split (Interior/Furniture), Featured Projects, Testimonials, Contact CTA.
- Interior Design Page: Filterable gallery (room type, style, color, budget).
- Furniture Design Page: Filterable gallery (product type, style, material, price).
- Project Detail Page: Image gallery, Before/After slider, Project specs, CTA.
- Testimonials Page: Categorized list of reviews.
- About Us Page: Story, Team, Partners.
- Contact Page: Form, WhatsApp integration, Maps.
- Admin Dashboard: CRUD for projects and testimonials.

Design Style: Premium, modern, minimal, high-end aesthetic.
Technical Stack: Next.js, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript.

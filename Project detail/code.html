The Obsidian Loft

Concept

The Art of Shadow Play

The Obsidian Loft is an exercise in atmospheric tension. By utilizing a monochromatic foundation, we allowed the natural Milanese light to dictate the mood of the space. Every surface was chosen for its tactile response to illumination.

Rather than relying on ornamentation, the project finds its voice through the juxtaposition of raw and refined textures. Shadow is not treated as an absence of light, but as a primary material in itself, carving out depth in the expansive atrium.
